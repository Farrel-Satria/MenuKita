# MenuKita Design System

Dokumen ringkas untuk memastikan konsistensi UI MenuKita di semua layar.

## Warna Utama
- Primary: `#FF6B35`
- Secondary: `#2ECC71`
- Accent: `#FFC107`
- Background: `#F9F7F4`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography
- Display: 28sp, bold
- Headline: 22sp, semibold
- Title: 18sp, medium
- Body: 14sp, regular
- Label: 12sp, medium

## Spacing (8dp scale)
- `2, 4, 6, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24, 28, 32, 40`

## Radius
- Small 10dp, Medium 14dp, Large 18dp, XL 24dp

## Elevation
- XS 1dp, SM 2dp, MD 4dp, LG 8dp

## Components
- Button: Filled/Outlined/Text (Material 3)
- Card: rounded, elevation halus
- Input: Outlined box, radius 14dp
- Chip: pill, stroke 1dp
